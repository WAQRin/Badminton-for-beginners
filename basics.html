Basics of Badminton

Equipment

To play badminton you obviously need it's suitable equipment. Such as a racket, shuttlecocks, proper shoes, and a court to play on or just a net.

Yes, they could get pricey, but playing with proper equipment genuinely helps the experience.

Court Layout

The badminton court has a net in the middle that divides the court equally and specific lines for singles and doubles.

Basic Rules

Some key rules for beginners:

  • Serve on the right side of the court if team's score is even and left side if it's odd.
  • A Fault is considered the shuttle is dead. Hence when its called whatever is perfomred afterwards is not counted.
  • Game is played up to 21 points. Win by at least 2 points closing to 21.
  • Do not hit shuttle when its out or after it has hit the ground.
