Q: What equipment do I need to start playing badminton?
A: At minimum, you'll need a racket, shuttlecocks, and proper footwear. A net is required for court play.
Q: How do I choose the right badminton racket?
A: Beginners should start with a lightweight racket with medium flexibility for better control.
Q: How is scoring done in badminton?
A: Matches are typically played to 21 points, with rally scoring. Players must win by 2 points.
Q: How often should I practice as a beginner?
A: Short, regular practice sessions 2-3 times a week are ideal for building skills without fatigue.
Q: Can I play badminton indoors and outdoors?
A: Yes, but indoor courts are preferred because wind can affect shuttlecock flight outdoors.
